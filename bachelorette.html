Part Three — The Bar

Build your bar.

Filter by what you're serving tonight — gin classics or soda & mocktails. The shopping list updates to match.

Gin Classics

One bottle, three parties.

Gin & Tonic

balloon glass
The unkillable classic. 30 seconds, no excuses.
  • Good gin (Hendrick's, Tanqueray)50 ml
  • Tonic water, chilled150 ml
  • Fresh lime, squeezed + dropped in1 wedge
  • Cucumber ribbon OR grapefruit peel1
  • Big ice cubes — fill the glass

French 75

champagne flute
The bachelorette drink. Dressed up, easy to batch.
  • Gin30 ml
  • Fresh lemon juice15 ml
  • Simple syrup (1:1 sugar+water)15 ml
  • Champagne or prosecco, top90 ml
  • Shake gin/lemon/syrup with ice → strain → top with bubbles
  • Long lemon twist1

Negroni

rocks glass
Bitter, elegant, makes you look like you know things.
  • Gin30 ml
  • Campari30 ml
  • Sweet vermouth (Carpano / Cinzano)30 ml
  • Stir with ice for 20 seconds
  • Orange peel, expressed over top1

Soda & Mocktails

Sprite, Fresca, ginger ale — zero excuses to skip.

Shirley Temple

highball glass
The original party starter. Pink, sweet, everyone loves it.
  • Sprite or 7UP, chilled180 ml
  • Grenadine syrup2 tbsp
  • Fresh orange juice (optional)30 ml
  • Ice cubesfull glass
  • Maraschino cherry + orange slice, garnish1 each
  • Pour grenadine first — it sinks for a sunrise effect

Fresca Paloma

rocks glass
Grapefruity, tart, and dangerously refreshing.
  • Fresca (grapefruit soda), chilled200 ml
  • Fresh lime juice30 ml
  • Pinch of salt or Tajín on the rim
  • Ice cubesfull glass
  • Grapefruit wedge, garnish1
  • Rim the glass, add ice, pour Fresca, squeeze lime in

Cucumber Mint Spritz

wine glass
Looks spa-fancy, tastes incredible, done in 2 minutes.
  • Sprite or lemon soda, chilled180 ml
  • Fresh cucumber slices3–4
  • Fresh mint leaves, muddled6–8
  • Fresh lime juice15 ml
  • Ice cubesfull glass
  • Muddle mint + cucumber with lime → add ice → top with Sprite

Gin Bar Stock-Up

3 drinks · one shopping list.

Bottles
  • Gin × 1 bottle (Hendrick's, Tanqueray, or Bombay)
  • Campari × 1 (200 ml is plenty)
  • Sweet vermouth × 1 (Carpano Antica or Cinzano Rosso)
  • Champagne or prosecco × 1 bottle (for the 75)
  • Tonic water × 2 large bottles (Fever-Tree if you can)
Fresh & Pantry
  • Fresh lemons × 4 (juice + twists for the 75)
  • Fresh oranges × 2 (peel for the Negroni)
  • Fresh limes × 3 (for G&T wedges)
  • Cucumber × 1 (for G&T ribbons)
  • White sugar × small bag (simple syrup: 1:1 sugar+hot water)
  • Big bag of ice — clear or large cubes ideal
  • Cocktail shaker + bar spoon + strainer

Soda Bar Stock-Up

3 mocktails · one shopping list.

Sodas
  • Sprite or 7UP × 2 large bottles (chilled)
  • Fresca (grapefruit soda) × 2 cans or 1 bottle
  • Grenadine syrup × 1 bottle (Rose's or Stirrings)
Fresh & Pantry
  • Fresh limes × 4 (Paloma + Spritz juice)
  • Fresh oranges × 2 (Shirley Temple garnish)
  • Cucumber × 1 (Mint Spritz slices)
  • Fresh mint × 1 bunch (Mint Spritz muddling)
  • Maraschino cherries × 1 jar (Shirley Temple)
  • Tajín or salt (Paloma rim, optional)
  • Big bag of ice + muddler
